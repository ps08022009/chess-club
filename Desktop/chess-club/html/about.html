About Our Chess Club

Welcome to our vibrant chess community! We are passionate about bringing people together through the timeless game of chess and meaningful connections.

♟️ Our Mission

To create an inclusive environment where members can learn, grow, and build lasting friendships while mastering the art of chess strategy and tactics.

🎯 Our Vision

To be the leading chess community that fosters intellectual development and strategic thinking through tournaments, workshops, and friendly competition.

📚 Our Journey

2020

Club Founded

Started with just 5 passionate chess players in a small classroom

2021

First Tournament

Hosted our inaugural campus chess championship with 25 participants

2022

Regional Success

Won the state collegiate chess championship and grew to 50 members

2023

Community Impact

Launched chess tutoring program for local schools, now 100+ active members
